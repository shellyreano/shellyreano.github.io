About Me

Hi, I'm Shelly — a 21-year-old BSIT student from the Polytechnic University of the Philippines, Sto. Tomas Branch. I’m an aspiring graphic designer who loves turning imagination into digital reality.

I enjoy creating designs that tell stories, spark emotions, and leave lasting impressions. I’m driven by curiosity and the excitement of learning something new every day. Whether I’m sketching concepts, coding web pages, or exploring photography, I always aim to blend creativity and innovation in everything I do.

My dream is to design experiences that inspire — not just visuals, but feelings that connect people.
